Our 7-phase Web Design Process

## A team you can count on.   A process you can trust.

### Phase 1 Onboarding

We set up your project management tools and kick things off with a meeting you’ll actually look forward to. The agenda? We’re discussing project details, your custom questionnaire, and project goals. And probably pets.

![Onboarding phase](https://connectivewebdesign.com/wp-content/uploads/Onboarding-phase.webp)

### Phase 2 Architecture

We’ll solidify your website’s sitemap and architecture, create wireframe designs for all templates, and gather website assets. (Already sweating? Don’t worry—we’ll explain every single step.)

![Architecture phase](https://connectivewebdesign.com/wp-content/uploads/Architecture-phase-versions.webp)

### Phase 3 Web design

Time for another meeting, but no new faces! The team you’re already coming to know and love will walk you through the Design Strategy. Then we’ll complete the internal design and review stages, and present you with (drum rolls not required, but always encouraged)… your web page designs!

![Web design phase](https://connectivewebdesign.com/wp-content/uploads/Web-design-phase-new-version.webp)

### Phase 4 Web development

Now, the techy stuff. We’ll be working on the back end to install WordPress and the necessary plugins, getting your design templates implemented, and adding all your content (web enhancements included!). It’s all coming together!

![Development phase](https://connectivewebdesign.com/wp-content/uploads/Development-phase-new.webp)

### Phase 5 QA & pre-launch

If “connection” wasn’t already our whole thing, it’d 100% be “quality.” We thoroughly review every aspect of your site with our detailed QA process—including mobile testing!—and our team reviews the website to ensure we’re hitting every design standard. Quality? Officially assured.

![QA and pre launch phase](https://connectivewebdesign.com/wp-content/uploads/QA-and-pre-launch-phase-new-version.webp)

### Phase 6 Launch & training

You’re celebrating, we’re celebrating… Your website is launched! We now conduct our final testing and wrap things up by sharing customized training and support on how to use and update your new website—explained in a way even those “allergic to technology” friends can understand, guaranteed.

![Launch and training phase ](https://connectivewebdesign.com/wp-content/uploads/Launch-and-training-phase-new-version.webp)

### Phase 7 Maintenance

Leaving you hanging just isn’t our style—that’s why our solutions don’t stop at launch. You can count on us for ongoing maintenance and support for your new website, including updates, backups, security measures, and more. We’re here to make your website something you never have to worry about. (Yes, we heard that huge sigh of relief!)
